Copenhagen Cocoa

iOS and OS X developer community in Copenhagen

NSCoder Nights – weekly

Informal meet-up where we discuss our projects and exchange ideas. Wednesdays at 19:00 in the Café Retro Nørrebro on Jægersborsgade 14.

CocoaHeads – bimonthly

Formal meet-ups with 2-3 talks, creators moments, and usually free snacks and beer.
